## Script Variables
* `Means_SDs`: Creates a filter for the columns that describe the activty, subject and have the words mean or std in them
* `Means_SDs2` : Applies the above filter to the merged dataset
* `GroupedbyActivity` : Takes the above data frame and organizes it by the Activity category
* `Cleaned` : Produces the mean of each of the filtered and sorted columns

## Data Identifier Column Names

* `subject` - The ID of the test subject
* `activity` - The type of activity performed when the corresponding measurements were taken

## Data Activity Label Column Names

* `WALKING` (flagged as 1): subject was walking during the test
* `WALKING_UPSTAIRS` (flagged as 2): subject was walking up a staircase during the test
* `WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS` (flagged as 3): subject was walking down a staircase during the test
* `SITTING` (flagged as 4): subject was sitting during the test
* `STANDING` (flagged as 5): subject was standing during the test
* `LAYING` (flagged as 6): subject was laying down during the test
